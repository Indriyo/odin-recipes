Brownies

Description

This easy brownie recipe takes minutes to whip up in one bowl and about 20 minutes to bake for a moist and fudgy crowd-pleasing treat!

Ingredients

  • baking spray
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup unsalted buter, melted
  • 4 large eggs, room temperature
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup walnut halves

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch pan with baking spray.
  3. Whisk sugar, flour, melted butter, eggs, cocoa powder, vanilla, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl until combined.
  4. Spread the batter into the prepared pan.
  5. Decorate with walnut halves.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until top is crinkled and a toothpick comes out with a few moist crumbs, about 20 to 30 minutes.
  7. Let cool completely in the pan on a wire rack before slicing into squares. Enjoy!
